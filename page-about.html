ABOUT US

ABOUT PLASTIKAMA INDUSTRY TECHNOLOGY

Since 2002, PT. Plastikatama Teknologi Industri specializes in environmental engineering, offering a wide range of services and solutions to address the challenges facing our planet today. We are involved in providing services in areas of Air pollution control, Water and wastewater treatment, Solid waste management, Environmental Remediation, Sustainable Development. The management of the company is committed to providing the highest quality of product and services. In order to keep pace with ever-changing technologies and customer needs, PT. Plastikatama Teknologi Industri has joined forces with some International companies to obtain technology that is not locally available whilst expanding its capabilities. The technical expertise on board allows our company to design the highest quality of products whilst maintaining competitive pricing. We are committed to making the world a better place by making sure our products reduce pollution, thus the existence of our trademark, “ENVICARE”.

UNDER HIGHEST QUALITY CIRCLE

Plastikatama Teknologi Industri are committed to meeting the highest quality of standards whilst providing after-sales-service.

UNDER INTERNATIONAL REPUTATION

Plastikatama Teknologi Industri is worldly renowned for its past projects around the world. It’s extensive networks and projects throughout the world provide credibility like none other.

ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY

As a core principle of practice, Plastikatama Teknologi Industri is here to care about the environment. We keep our practices and services environmentally friendly.

EXPERIENCED

We have dealt with 1600+ projects of different fields facing different types of clients. We are highly experienced in delivering timely services whilst having the flexibility to adjust our products and services according to the customer’s request.
